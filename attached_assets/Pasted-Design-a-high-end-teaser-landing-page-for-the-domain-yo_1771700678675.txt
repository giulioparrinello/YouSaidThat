Design a high-end teaser landing page for the domain “yousaidthat.org”.

This is NOT the full product website. It is a teaser page meant to build credibility, curiosity and authority around a privacy-first, trustless future prediction platform.

The aesthetic should feel comparable to modern, premium tech startups like resend.com, linear.app, or superhuman.com.

GENERAL ART DIRECTION

• Extremely clean layout
• Strong typography hierarchy
• Generous whitespace
• Soft motion and subtle interaction
• No gimmicks, no loud gradients
• Calm, intelligent, future-facing

COLOR PALETTE

Primary: Off-white (#FAFAFA) or very light gray background  
Text: Near-black (#111111)  
Secondary text: Neutral gray (#666666)  
Accent: Deep electric blue (#3B82F6) OR muted indigo (#6366F1)  
Optional subtle glow effects using very soft radial gradients in the background.

TYPOGRAPHY

Primary font: Modern geometric sans-serif (Inter, Satoshi, or similar).
Headlines: Bold, tight letter spacing, large scale.
Body: Medium weight, comfortable reading width (max 600px).
Small caps or mono font for technical hints (optional subtle detail).

HERO SECTION

Large headline:

“Your Predictions. Your Proof.”

Subheadline:
“Record your ideas about the future. Seal them in time. Reveal them when the year arrives.”

CTA Button:
Primary: “Create a Prediction”
Secondary (ghost style): “Learn More”

The CTA should have:
• Soft hover animation
• Slight lift effect on hover
• Subtle glow or border animation

BACKGROUND

Very subtle animated abstract pattern:
• Floating dots connected by thin lines
OR
• Soft moving particles
OR
• A minimal time-wave animation

The motion must be calm, almost imperceptible.

CONCEPT SECTION

Three horizontally aligned blocks (or grid on mobile):

1. Privacy-first
Short copy:
“Your words are encrypted before they leave your device.”

2. Immutable Proof
Short copy:
“Every prediction is cryptographically timestamped.”

3. Reveal in the Future
Short copy:
“Unlock your statement when the year comes.”

Icons:
Simple, thin-line SVG icons.
Monochrome, subtle animation on hover (stroke draw or fade).

VISUAL DETAIL ELEMENTS

• Thin horizontal dividers
• Soft shadow on cards (very subtle)
• Micro animations on scroll (fade-up 100ms stagger)
• Minimalistic footer

FOOTER

Centered:
yousaidthat.org
© 2026
Twitter / GitHub (non-functional placeholders)

OPTIONAL PREMIUM DETAIL

Include a small interactive demo preview:
A fake “prediction capsule” card in the center with:
• Year: 2040
• Status: Locked
• Keywords: AI / Energy / Society
With a subtle lock icon and hover glow.

RESPONSIVENESS

Fully responsive.
Mobile should stack vertically.
Maintain whitespace and hierarchy.

TONE

Professional.
Calm.
Forward-looking.
No hype language.
No buzzwords like “revolutionary” or “disruptive.”

The page should feel like:
“A serious infrastructure project is coming.”

Not:
“A crypto toy project.”
